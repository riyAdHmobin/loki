# Credits

The sprite sheets under `assets/skins/` are generated placeholder art
(`python -m deskcats.gen_placeholder_art`, see `deskcats/gen_placeholder_art.py`),
not hand-drawn or licensed assets.

They exist so the engine can be developed and tested without needing real
art up front. Replace them with a recolored CC0 sprite pack before release,
and record that pack's license/attribution here.
